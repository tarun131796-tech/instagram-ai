(If post includes images or carousel slides)

For each image:

- Style: (e.g. lifestyle photography, studio product shot)
- Lighting: (soft daylight, indoor diffused, etc.)

Lighting: Soft natural daylight
Mood: Clean, calm, premium
Aspect Ratio: 1:1
